Healthy Kids Repository-India

Why?

A healthy child's development actually begins before conception with the parents' health and their genetic legacy. Unfortunately, even the healthiest baby can get sick. It is worth knowing the signs and symptoms of the common childhood illnesses as well as the treatment and prevention of these illnesses.

How?

We provide a Kids Development Monitoring System to keeps track of kids Sleeping, Growing (Height, Weight, Head circumference) and Vaccinations. e.g. Any Vaccinations for any diseases taken (e.g. Polio dose).
